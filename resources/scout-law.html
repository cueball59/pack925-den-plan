The Scout Law

Cub Scout Pack 925

A Scout is…

TrustworthyI tell the truth and keep my promises.
LoyalI stand by my family, friends, and country.
HelpfulI help other people every day.
FriendlyI am a friend to everyone I meet.
CourteousI use good manners and say please and thank you.
KindI am gentle and care about others and animals.
ObedientI follow the rules of my family, school, and pack.
CheerfulI smile and look for the good in things.
ThriftyI take care of my things and do not waste.
BraveI do the right thing even when it is hard.
CleanI keep my body, words, and space clean.
ReverentI respect my beliefs and the beliefs of others.
