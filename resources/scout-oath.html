The Scout Oath

Cub Scout Pack 925

On my honor I will do my best
to do my duty to God and my country
and to obey the Scout Law;
to help other people at all times;
to keep myself physically strong,
mentally awake, and morally straight.

Duty to God & Country

I love and help take care of my family, my faith, and my country.

Help Other People

I do a Good Turn every day and help people at all times.

Keep Myself Strong

Strong body, sharp mind, and always doing what is right.

✌️ Give the Cub Scout sign when you say the Oath!
Do Your Best! · Say it with your right hand raised in the Cub Scout sign.
